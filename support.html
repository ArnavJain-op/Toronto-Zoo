Every donation counts and has an impact

Toronto Zoo plays an active and crucial role in global conservation.

The Zoo is home to over 100 threatened species, including some that are Extinct-in-the-Wild, relying on care within the Zoo to survive for future generations.

As part of ZSL, the science-driven, wildlife conservation charity, we rely on donations, members and visitors to help us care for our animals and restore wildlife around the world.

How to donate to Toronto Zoo

Join our fight for wildlife with a monthly donation

Each month we’ll update you on the successes you’ve made possible with fascinating personal stories from our Zookeepers, Conservationists, and Scientists, showing how your donations are making a difference.

Toronto Zoo is part of ZSL, an international science-driven conservation charity, working to restore wildlife in the UK and around the world. Also visit Whipsnade Zoo, a ZSL conservation zoo.
