Finding your way around the Zoo

Use our map to find your favourite animals around the Zoo. The zoo is split into three zones, orange, blue, and pink, to help you stay on track during your wildlife adventure.

You can also download the Toronto Zoo mobile app. Explore the conservation Zoo your way with GPS enabled step by step navigation, create a plan for your day, view animal information, daily talks schedule, and so much more!

Habitats

Experience wildlife from across the world in a day like no other, hopping from the lush rainforests of South America to the mountainous monkey-filled jungle of Central Africa.

Animals

Get closer to nature and meet our amazing animals, from tiny marching leafcutter ants to a giant 193kg silverback gorilla.

Accessibility

Our commitment to accessibility has us working with local people and specialist organisations to learn how we can improve, and make positive changes to ensure every visitor has a wonderful day out.

Toronto Zoo is part of ZSL, an international science-driven conservation charity, working to restore wildlife in the UK and around the world. Also visit Whipsnade Zoo, a ZSL conservation zoo.
